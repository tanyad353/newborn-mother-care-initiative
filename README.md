# With Heart and Hands

## About

The Newborn Care Initiative is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting new mothers and their newborn babies by providing essential care kits through fundraising and community involvement.

I am a Co-Founder of this organization. Through this project, I have helped organize fundraising events, assemble care kits, and coordinate donations to local hospitals.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide practical support and comfort to mothers and newborns by supplying thoughtfully assembled care kits while encouraging community service and volunteerism.

---

## What We've Accomplished

- Organized multiple fundraising events
- Raised money to purchase supplies for care kits
- Assembled care kits with volunteers
- Donated completed care kits to Beth Israel Hospital
- Continued expanding our community outreach

---

## Recent Milestone

### Beth Israel Hospital Care Kit Delivery
